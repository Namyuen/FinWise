About FinWise

FinWise was created to improve financial literacy among students by making money education accessible, practical, and engaging.

The Problem

Every year, millions of high school students graduate and enter the real world without a fundamental understanding of how money works. They are handed credit cards without knowing how compound interest works, and take out massive student loans without understanding the long-term impact on their monthly budget.

Financial literacy isn't a core subject in most schools. The results are predictable: debt, stress, and missed opportunities.

Our Mission

We believe financial education should be free, clear, and practical. No jargon, no condescension, no get-rich-quick schemes. Just the solid, proven rules of money that wealthy people use every day.

FinWise is designed specifically for teenagers and young adults. We want to be the trusted older sibling who works in finance, explaining things clearly so you can avoid the mistakes we made.

How We Built This

FinWise is a completely free, client-side application. This means:

Privacy First: We don't save your data. Your budget inputs and quiz scores stay on your device.
Lightning Fast: No waiting for servers. Calculators update instantly as you type.
Modern Tech: Built using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with Chart.js for data visualization.

Future Plans

We are just getting started. In the future, we plan to add:

  • AI Finance Tutor: A chatbot that can answer specific questions about your financial situation.
  • User Accounts: The ability to opt-in to saving your progress, bookmarks, and custom budgets.
  • Mobile App: A dedicated native app for iOS and Android.
  • More Modules: Taxes deep-dive, crypto basics, and career income planning.
  • Gamification: Streaks, badges, and leaderboards to encourage daily learning.
